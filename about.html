● The story

Built inside real operations, not in a vacuum

MetricForge started with a simple frustration: operations teams generate mountains of data every day, and most of it dies in spreadsheets that take hours to update and minutes to ignore.

The tools here weren't designed as products first. Each one began as a fix for a real bottleneck inside a working operations team — unfair daily targets, reports that took half a day to assemble, recognition programs run on manual cross-checking. The versions sold here carry the same logic, rebuilt on demo data with clean, documented source code.

That origin shapes how everything is built: practical over impressive, explainable over clever, and always shipped with the source so you're never locked in.

01

Explainable by design

If a number can't be traced back to its inputs, people won't trust it. Every model here can be audited by the person it measures.

02

Source code, always

Templates come with the complete files and a setup guide. Customize freely — no locked features, no subscription hostage-taking.
